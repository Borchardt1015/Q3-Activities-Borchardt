What is "Fire Prevention Month"

This Year 2023 "Fire Prevention Month" is March and the purpose of Fire Prevention Month is to spread awareness about the dangers of fire's and how we can prevent them by pointing out the hazards and causes of them. We can prevent these terrible catastrophes with only simple steps such as using surge protectors, unpluging outlets that are not being used and keeping flammable materials away from where a fire would happen.
